Number of Avocado toasts to give up to pay a house deposit.

This critique is based on the following article by BBC titled "If young people stopped wasting their money on lavish things like toast, could they use that money to buy a house? We crunched the numbers to find out."

Critique on above Data Visual:

  1. I really liked the colour scheme of the visualisation. It seems relevant and the mildness makes it appealing.

  2. Although the graphics are great (for example city logos, the avo-toasts) their overuse lead to distraction. I would make the numbers more attractive so the user doesn't have to look for the numerical information.

  3. The information redundancy is another drawback (2 sentences repeated 5 times).

  4. 5 different graphs instead of one make comparison difficult.
